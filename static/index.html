How To Read Your Grade

This grade measures real-time structural quality — trend, volume, momentum, and volatility. Here's what it means for your style of trading.

DAY TRADER

⚡ Built for you

This grade is computed from the same intraday engine that powers our automated trading bot. An "A" or "B" grade means the stock has the structural conditions right now for a directional move — volume is confirming, trend is aligned, and the entry zone is active.

A / B Grade → Actively look for entries
D / F Grade → Stay away — something is broken
SCALPER

🎯 Use it as a filter

The grade tells you which stocks to sit on, not where to enter. High-graded tickers have the ATR (range) and RVOL (liquidity) you need for clean fills and meaningful price movement. Use it to build your morning watchlist, then execute on your own charts.

A / B Grade → Add to scalp watchlist
D / F Grade → No range, no volume — skip it

📊 Use it as confirmation

This grade is optimized for intraday conditions, not multi-day holds. But the EMA ribbon, relative strength, and volume signals are relevant to your thesis. If you already have a daily chart setup and the Ticker Grader also grades it highly on entry day — that's confluence.

A / B Grade → Confirms your daily setup
